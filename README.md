## 📌 Project Overview

The Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI. This project analyzes food delivery operations, customer behavior, restaurant performance, and revenue trends to help stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard is divided into three analytical sections:

* Executive Overview
* Customer Analytics
* Restaurant Performance Analytics

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall business performance
* Analyze customer retention and ordering behavior
* Identify top-performing restaurants
* Evaluate cuisine-wise revenue contribution
* Track delivery efficiency and operational KPIs
* Support strategic decision-making through data visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Key Metrics:

* Total Revenue: 165M
* Total Orders: 200K
* Average Order Value: 825
* Average Delivery Time: 44.55 Minutes

Visualizations:

* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Order Trend Analysis
* Order Status Breakdown
* Revenue by City
* Top Restaurants by Revenue

Visualizations:

### 3. Restaurant Performance Analytics

## 📈 Key Insights

* Generated more than 165M in revenue from 200K+ orders.
* Repeat customers contributed significantly to overall order volume.
* Certain cuisines generated substantially higher revenue than others.
* Restaurant ratings showed a positive relationship with revenue performance.
* Delivery efficiency played a key role in customer retention.
